About Us

Discover who we are, what we believe, and what we do

Our Mission

The Pathfinder Club is a church-centered spiritual-recreational-activity program designed for young people ages 10-15. We aim to develop Christian character, leadership skills, and a love for God and nature through hands-on activities and community service.

Our mission is to help every Pathfinder:

  • Develop a personal relationship with Jesus Christ
  • Build strong character and moral values
  • Learn practical life and survival skills
  • Develop leadership abilities
  • Serve their community with compassion

Our Vision

We envision a generation of young people who are:

  • Spiritually grounded in their faith
  • Physically active and healthy
  • Mentally sharp and curious learners
  • Socially responsible and compassionate
  • Prepared to make positive contributions to society

Through adventure, service, and spiritual growth, we strive to equip Pathfinders with the tools they need to navigate life's challenges and become leaders in their communities.
